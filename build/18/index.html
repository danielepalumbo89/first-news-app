Faithful Word Baptist Church

The Faithful Word Baptist Church group is located in Tempe, AZ. It's a Anti-LGBT group. Opposition to equal rights for the LGBT community has been a key rallying point for the Christian right in the US for decades. The SPLC lists several groups who it claims stray into hate speech, and "engage in crude name-calling and disseminate disparaging propaganda and falsehoods about this population, such as the claim that gay men molest children at vastly higher rates than straight men.".
